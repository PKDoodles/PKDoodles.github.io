Overview

Here is all my Graphic Design work I achieved through A-Level projects and Personal Enjoyment! Most of this work was achieved through a mixture of Photoshop and Krita, primarily consists of three Projects:

⦿ Reality Check - A video game concept designed around street life and parkour, focusing of bright colours and sharp edges
⦿ Cosmic Cafe - A restaurant project with a primary concept of mixing night time neon with deserts and healthy eating
⦿ Magic Shop Cheshire - An Alice in Wonderland inspired magic shop based around the Cheshire cat and mysterious smoke


These projects have greatly improved my proficiency in both 2D art, visual design and usage of creative softwares! I have learnt a lot about how to experiment with different ideas to create a consistent and interest brand image for a fun concept!

I now plan to pursue graphic design as a hobby and continue ot create fun projects and expand my skills in digital design and the creatiev process!
